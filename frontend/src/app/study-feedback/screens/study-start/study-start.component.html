Welcome to our evaluation study!

This study aims for understanding, how potential users interact with this prototype and whether it can help them give better feedback in typical scenarios of supervising software engineering student projects.

You will be presented a sequence of seven small tasks. Please solve these tasks using the information offered on this platform. You may exit the study at any given time, but please take notice, that a complete run of this study may produce the most reliable results.

The following study requires anonymized recording of relevant interaction events:

timeline navigation paths and dwell times list_alt evaluation system interaction dns system events mouse click and hover events schedule event timestamps

If you have any doubts later on, you may request deletion of your generated data. Simply send a mail to Felix Riedl

By clicking the BEGIN-button, you agree on the logging and submission of the previously mentioned events and start the study.
